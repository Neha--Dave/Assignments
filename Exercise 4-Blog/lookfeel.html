Look v/s Feel

You must be wondering as to why is the conjunction 'v/s' present instead of 'and'. It is just written to grab your attention towards the difference between structure and presentation. In technical terms HTML stands for structure whereas CSS stands for presentation. Structure is the logical organization of the content as opposed to presentation which is the use of visual characteristics to represent content. Take for example, you have a paragraph tag <p>. HTML tells you that it is the area to write your text whereas in CSS you will set its font, size or position.
